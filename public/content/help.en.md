## Adding Content

All content is encrypted on your device before it's stored. No one — including SeraVault — can read your files.

### Uploading Files

1. Click the **+** button (bottom-right corner) and select **Upload Files**
2. Choose one or more files from your device
3. Files are encrypted automatically before upload

You can also right-click anywhere in the sidebar's **All Files** area to upload.

### Creating a Form

Forms are structured records — great for passwords, IDs, bank accounts, and anything else with labelled fields.

1. Click **+** → **New Form**
2. The template picker opens — choose a built-in template or one of your own, or skip it to start blank
3. Fill in your fields and click **Save**

### Starting a Chat

Chats let you exchange encrypted messages and files with your contacts.

1. Click **+** → **New Chat**
2. Select one or more contacts to include
3. Type your message or attach a file

### Creating Folders

1. Click **+** → **New Folder**, or right-click a folder in the sidebar and choose **New Folder**
2. Name your folder and press **Enter**

## Organizing Your Vault

### Folders

The sidebar shows your folder tree. Click any folder to open it. Folders with subfolders have an expand arrow — click it to reveal them without navigating away.

### Moving Files and Folders

Drag any file or folder from the main content area and drop it onto a folder in the sidebar. You can also **Cut** an item and **Paste** it into a folder (see below).

### Cut, Copy & Paste

Right-click any file or folder to access Cut and Copy. Then navigate to the destination folder and right-click the sidebar background (or a folder in the sidebar) to Paste.

- **Cut** moves the item to the destination
- **Copy** creates a re-encrypted copy for the destination

### Favorites

Click the star icon on any file to mark it as a favorite. Access all favorites from **Favorites** in the sidebar. Favorites are private — they're only visible to you.

### Tags

Add tags to files to group them across folders. Filter your vault by tag using the tag panel on the left. Tags are encrypted and only visible to you.

### Recent Files

**Recents** in the sidebar shows the files you've opened most recently, sorted by last access time.

### Searching

Use the search bar at the top of the file list to filter by name within the current folder. For a global search across all your files, use the global search in the top bar.

### Renaming

Right-click a file or folder and select **Rename**. Your personal name is encrypted — only you see it, even for shared files.

### Archive & Delete

**To archive:**
1. Right-click the item → **Archive**
2. It moves to **Archive** in the sidebar and disappears from your main vault

**To restore:**
1. Open **Archive** in the sidebar
2. Right-click the item → **Restore**

**To permanently delete:**
1. Open **Archive** in the sidebar
2. Right-click the item → **Delete**
3. Confirm — this cannot be undone

> Files must be archived before they can be permanently deleted. This prevents accidents.

## Form Templates

Templates give your forms a consistent structure so you don't start from scratch each time.

### Built-in Templates

SeraVault includes ready-made templates for common vault items:

| Category | Templates |
|---|---|
| Credentials | Password, Passkey |
| Finance | Credit Card, Bank Account, Crypto Wallet |
| Identity | Personal ID, Passport, Driver's License, Social Security |
| Insurance | Health, Auto, Home/Renters, Life |
| Medical | Medical Record, Prescription, Vaccination Record |
| Property | Vehicle, Real Estate |
| Network | Wi-Fi Network |
| Legal & Work | Legal Document, Software License, Tax Document, Employment Record |
| Other | Emergency Contact, Custom |

### Creating Personal Templates

**From the form dialog:**
1. Click **+** → **New Form**
2. Go to the **Personal** tab
3. Click **Create Template** (top right) or **Create Your First Template**
4. Give the template a name, description, category, and icon
5. Add fields with **+ Add Field** — available types: text, number, date, password, email, URL, phone, textarea, checkbox
6. Mark sensitive fields as **Sensitive** to mask them by default
7. **Save Template**

**From the Templates page:**
1. Click **Templates** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Template** and follow the same steps

### Managing Templates

- **Edit or delete** templates from the Templates page
- Your personal templates appear in the **Personal** tab of the form dialog
- All template content — field names, descriptions, structure — is encrypted

## Contacts

You can only share files with people in your contacts. Both of you must accept the connection before sharing is possible.

### Adding a Contact

**If they already have a SeraVault account:**
1. Click **Contacts** in the sidebar → **Add Contact**
2. Enter their email address and an optional message
3. Click **Send Request**

They'll see your request in their **Requests** tab. Once they accept, you're connected.

**If they don't have an account yet:**
1. Follow the same steps and enter their email
2. SeraVault will open your email app with a pre-filled invitation
3. When they sign up using your link, you're automatically connected

Requests expire after 30 days if not accepted.

### Accepting a Request

1. Click **Contacts** → **Requests** tab
2. Click ✓ to accept or ✗ to decline

### Managing Contacts

- Search contacts by name or email from the Contacts page
- Use the menu on any contact to remove or block them

## Sharing

All shared content is re-encrypted for each recipient — only they can decrypt it.

### Sharing Files or Folders

1. Right-click the file or folder → **Share**
2. Select which contacts to share with
3. Each recipient gets their own encrypted copy in their **Shared with Me** view

Changes you make to a shared file are not automatically synced to recipients — sharing creates a snapshot at the time of sharing.

### Sharing Forms

Open a form, then click the **Share** button in the form toolbar. Select contacts to share with.

### Shared with Me

Click **Shared** in the sidebar to see all files others have shared with you.

### Chat Attachments

Inside a chat conversation, click the attachment icon to send a file. The file is encrypted for all participants in the conversation.

## Security

### How Encryption Works

Every file is encrypted on your device using your private key before it's uploaded. When you share a file, it's re-encrypted specifically for the recipient using their public key. The server only ever sees encrypted data — not your files, form values, folder names, or tags.

Your passphrase is never stored anywhere. It's used locally to unlock your private key, which stays on your device.

### Post-Quantum Protection

SeraVault uses **ML-KEM-768** (NIST-standardized post-quantum cryptography) for key encapsulation. This protects your data against future quantum computer attacks — not just today's threats.

## Profile & Key Management

Access your profile by clicking your avatar or name at the bottom of the sidebar.

### Passphrase

Your passphrase is the master key to your vault. It unlocks your private key each time you log in.

- **Change your passphrase** from Profile → Security tab
- There is no "forgot passphrase" — if you lose it and have no backup, your encrypted data cannot be recovered
- Write your passphrase down and store it somewhere safe offline

### Biometric & Device Unlock

Skip typing your passphrase by using your device's fingerprint, face scan, or PIN.

- Set up from **Profile → Security tab → Device Unlock**
- Biometric data never leaves your device
- Your passphrase remains as a backup if biometrics fail
- Register biometrics separately on each device you use

### Hardware Security Keys

Use a YubiKey or other FIDO2 device for the strongest possible unlock.

- Set up from **Profile → Security tab → Hardware Security Keys**
- Register multiple keys as a backup in case one is lost
- **Hybrid mode** (default): your key is protected by both your passphrase and hardware — either works
- **Hardware-only mode**: the passphrase backup is removed for maximum security; make sure you have a spare key registered before enabling this

### Exporting & Importing Your Key

**Export your private key** — a critical backup step:
1. Go to **Profile → Data & Backups**
2. Click **Download Recovery Key**
3. Store the file somewhere secure and offline — not in cloud storage

**Import a key** to recover access on a new device or after losing your passphrase:
1. Go to **Profile → Data & Backups**
2. Click **Import Private Key** and select your backup file

> Your exported key file contains your private key, but it is **encrypted with the passphrase you provide during export**. It is safe to store digitally, though offline storage is still recommended. Treat this file like a physical house key — keep it safe and never share it.

### Notifications

Enable push notifications from **Profile → Preferences** to be alerted when:
- A contact request arrives
- Someone shares a file with you
- A chat message arrives

### Account & Data

From **Profile → Data & Backups** you can:
- **Export all your data** as a local backup
- **Import from another password manager** (JSON format)

From **Profile → Security** you can:
- **Delete your account** permanently — this removes all your data from SeraVault's servers

## Tips & Shortcuts

- **Right-click folders** in the sidebar to create subfolders, paste, upload, create forms, or start chats — no need to use the FAB
- **Hover over a folder** in the sidebar before clicking to preload it instantly
- **Drag files** from the main area onto sidebar folders to move them
- **Multi-select** files in list view to cut, copy, or archive in bulk
- **Tags + filter** is a powerful alternative to deep folder hierarchies
- The **Recents** view is useful after a session break to get back to what you were working on
- If SeraVault has an update available, a notification bar will appear — you choose when to apply it
- Import progress for large batches continues even if you navigate to a different section
